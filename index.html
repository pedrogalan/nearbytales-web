№ 01 — How it works

A guidebook that reads itself.

01

It knows where you are

Quiet GPS listens for landmarks within your chosen radius — from 100 m to a kilometre.

02

It tells you the story

A short, well-written narration plays through your headphones. No swiping, no screens.

03

You keep walking

Phone in your pocket, eyes on the city. Stories pause and resume as you wander.
